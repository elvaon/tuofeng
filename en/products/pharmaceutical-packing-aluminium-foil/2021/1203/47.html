Description
The aluminum foil packaging has good light-shielding and compactness, and can protect the inner packaging medicine from oxidation, moisture absorption and deterioration, and has good calendering property and is easy to produce and easy to disassemble. After compounding, printing and gluing, it is widely used in the packaging of various medicine capsules, tablets and granules.
